Who is Rapid Revive?

We are a 100% Employee-Owned company that has been serving our Socal community for over a decade with deep roots dating back to 1970. Our vast experience, workmanship, and commitment to quality is how we provide our clients a peace of mind.

Design

We design with purpose - Every project starts with a vision. Our experienced team collaborates closely with clients to understand their goals and aspirations, ensuring efficiences and anticipating issues.

Build

We build with precision - Our team bring the design to life with quality materials and proven construction methods. We take ownership and pride in every project and deliver high quality projects.

Nuture

We nuture what we create - we prioritize communication, responsiveness, and trust. Our goal is not just to deliver a successful project, but to be a contractor you can rely on for years to come.

Construction is what we do.
Excellence is how we do it.

The way we work matters. By self-performing our projects, we maintain quality, consistency, and accountability at every stage. This approach allows us to overcome challenges together and create meaningful outcomes for our clients, our team, and the communities we support.
